SYMPOSIUM

The symposium at Amalthea is an opportunity for academicians, industry leaders, policy-makers and technology pioneers to collaborate, discuss, and debate some of the most pressing technology-related issues, educating participants about the intricacies of these subjects and inspiring them to contribute to these causes themselves. The theme for this year's symposium is titled '5G in India: What Lies Ahead.'

Why '5G in India: What Lies Ahead'?

5G will revolutionise the digital economy and alter how we interact with the world. It will open up an infinite number of possibilities. However, it is also imperative that technology is used in a safe manner and that it is actually used to empower people. The symposium aims to bring together experts from industry, academia, and government to collaborate and discuss potential solutions these pressing issues.
